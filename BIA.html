August 1, 2025

Brother Of The Month

The brother of the month for August is Justin "Double Barrel" Harril. Justin is a Criminal Justice major from Lawton Oklahoma. He is in ROTC and plans to commission as an officer in the army when he graduates. Justin also serves as BIA chair, and recently attended the 75th Biennial Grand Conclave in Las Vegas.
"I'm blessed to be a part of a fraternity that makes me feel like I truly belong. No matter what happens, I know I can count on my brothers to have my back."
- Justin Harril
